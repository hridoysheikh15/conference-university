For Authors

Author Guidelines

Everything you need to know before preparing and submitting your extended abstract.

Abstract Length & Format

Extended abstracts should be 300–500 words, submitted as a single PDF or Word file following the official template.

Official Template

Download and use the official ICP 2026 abstract template — submissions in other formats will not be reviewed.

Submission Platform

All abstracts are submitted online through the conference's abstract submission portal — no email submissions accepted.

Review Process

Every submission undergoes double-blind peer review by the technical committee before acceptance.

Presentation Mode

Accepted authors may present as an oral talk or a poster, as indicated at the time of submission.

Plagiarism Policy

Submissions are screened for originality; plagiarized or previously published content will be rejected without review.
